Things to look for in this website

  • Plenty of validation

    - Checks your login information

    - Checks if your email has already been taken by someone else

    - Checks to see if your passwords are the same on the sign up page

    - Checks to see if a username has already been taken

  • Security is key

    - Your password is hashed (with salt added) before being stored in our database, so there is no way we or any other hacker can know your password!

  • Completely editable

    - Enjoy the freedom to create, delete, or edit your account, any article you write, or your account information

  • Searching is easy

    - In the easy to use search bar, all you have to do is type a key phrase, and a list of matching articles are returned

    - The matching phrase is highlighted in either the title and/or content of the returned articles...CASE INSENSITIVE

    - All of the returned articles ARE CLICKABLE and you can view and edit any article you please

  • Making articles stylish

    - When you enter text in a textarea (such as the create article page), your new lines and paragraphs are taken into account and will be displayed when the article is saved

    - You are also free to add tags of your choice such as <b> or <i> to style your text. It will be displayed when your article is saved
